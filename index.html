About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Scientist in the Lightwave Research Laboratory at Columbia University, New York, NY, USA, supervised by Prof. Keren Bergman. My research interests include the modeling, design, and optimization of ultra-broadband, energy-efficient, and reconfigurable optical interconnects and systems for data-centric applications.

Prior to joining Columbia, I earned my M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of California, Santa Barbara, CA, USA, in 2018 and 2021, respectively. I earned my B.Eng. degree in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2015.

🕵️‍♂️ I am seeking tenure-track faculty positions starting in 2024. Feel free to contact me at yw3831 [at] columbia [dot] edu.
