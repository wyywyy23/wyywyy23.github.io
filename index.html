About Me

Yuyang Wang is a Postdoctoral Research Scientist in the Lightwave Research Laboratory at Columbia University, New York, NY, USA, supervised by Prof. Keren Bergman. His research interests include the modeling, design, and optimization of ultra-broadband, energy-efficient, and reconfigurable optical interconnects and systems for data-centric applications. Prior to joining Columbia, he earned his Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of California, Santa Barbara, CA, USA, in 2021.
